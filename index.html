What happens next:

After the Assembly Floor vote on May 19 or May 26, AB 2158 will cross over to the Senate. It will be heard in the Senate Education Committee, then the Senate Appropriations Committee, and then the full Senate Floor before heading to the Governor, who has 30 days to sign it. We will share clear updates and action steps at each stage.
